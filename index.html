About Us

At GoKool Tinting, we specialize in high-quality residential, and commercial window tinting services. Founded with a passion for heat reduction, UV protection, and enhanced privacy, our team delivers top-tier results. Our experts use premium ceramic and carbon films to ensure durability and performance. We serve our local community with fast, reliable installations and exceptional customer care. Our mission is to help clients enjoy cooler interiors, lower energy bills, and reduced glare all year round. With years of experience, we’ve perfected techniques that boost comfort while protecting furnishings and your home interiors. At GoKool, we’re committed to eco‑friendly solutions, using films that block up to 99% of harmful UV rays. Every project begins with a free consultation to match client needs with optimal tinting options. Our residential service enhances comfort and curb appeal for homes and offices alike. For business owners, our commercial window films improve energy efficiency and reinforce building security. We stand behind our work with industry-leading warranties and durable, long-lasting films. Each installation follows strict quality control, ensuring seamless application and superior finish. We maintain full transparency in pricing—no hidden fees, just clear, competitive quotes. Our customer-focused approach includes flexible scheduling and fast turnarounds.

Services

We provide professional window tinting services for residential, commercial needs. Enhance comfort, reduce heat, and protect interiors with our high-quality, long-lasting films.

Residential Window Tinting

Keep your home cooler, protect interiors from UV damage, and enjoy daytime privacy with residential window tinting. Our films blend seamlessly with your windows, offering performance without compromising aesthetics.

Commercial Window Tinting

Boost productivity, reduce energy costs, and improve privacy with our commercial window tinting solutions. Perfect for offices, showrooms, and retail spaces looking to enhance comfort and style.

Security Films

Our high-strength security window films add an extra layer of protection against break-ins, accidents, and severe weather. They hold shattered glass in place, enhancing safety and peace of mind for homes and businesses.

Features

Heat & Glare Reduction

Window tinting significantly reduces solar heat gain, keeping indoor spaces cooler and more comfortable. It also minimizes glare on screens and surfaces, improving productivity and relaxation

UV Protection

Tinted windows block up to 99% of harmful UV rays, protecting furniture, flooring, artwork, and skin from sun damage and fading.

Energy Efficiency

By reducing the need for air conditioning, window tinting lowers electricity bills and improves the building’s energy performance, making it an eco-friendly upgrade.

Privacy & Security

Tinted films enhance privacy during the day without sacrificing natural light. Some films also provide shatter-resistance, improving glass strength and security.
